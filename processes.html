Which peace processes have the most influence on a constitution?

To qualify for inclusion in the analysis a peace process must contain at least one agreement with provisions that map onto constitution sections.

Percentage of constitution sections mapped by provisions

The plot below shows the percentage of constitution sections that are mapped by provisions of the agreements of a peace process. The majority of the sections of the Bosnia & Herzegovina constitution are mapped by provisions and the Dayton agrrement contained the proposed constitution so it's not surprising that Bosnia & Herzegovina process highest. The Chechnya process is high because of the presence of the term Russian Federation in provisions and sections in Russia (Russia_2014).
