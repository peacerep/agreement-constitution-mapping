Is there a relationship between the stage of an agreement and the likelihood that the agreement contains provisions that map onto sections of a future constitution?

There are 1447 agreements in the mapping analysis. Of these 1447 agreements, 985 precede an in-force constitution.

Of the 985 agreements that precede an in-force constitution, 418 contain provisions that map onto sections of the constitution. These 418 agreements are referred to as mapping agreements. The remaining 567 agreements are the non-mapping agreements.
