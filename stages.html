Is there a relationship between the stage of an agreement and the likelihood that the agreement contains provisions that map onto sections of a future constitution?

There are 1447 agreements in the mapping analysis. Of these 1447 agreements, 1080 precede an in-force constitution. None of these 1080 agreements precede an earlier constitution.

Of the 1080 agreements that precede an in-force constitution, 518 contain provisions that map onto sections of a future constitution. These 518 agreements are referred to as mapping agreements. The remaining 562 agreements are the non-mapping agreements.
